Rikishi

Rikishi are Japanese sumo wrestlers. If you make two of these, you can have a sumo match with your friend! Let's give it a try!

  1. Fold the paper in two in the middle and fold it in two again.
  2. Fold the four corners inward.
  3. Turn the paper over and fold it from left to right toward the center.
  4. Turn the paper over again, spread the bottom part and fold the top part.
  5. Turn over and fold in the top, then reopen the folded part.
  6. Fold in half toward the center line and form the back belt, and you are done!
