Crane

Crane Origami is the most popular one in Japan. Let's try!

  1. Fold the paper in two in the middle and fold it in two again.
  2. Fold the paper open to form a rectangle, then fold the other side in the same way.
  3. Fold in to form a right triangle crease, then unfold alongthe crease to form a rhombus shape.
  4. Do the same for the other side as in step 3.
  5. Fold both sides toward the center and do the same on the other side.
  6. Fold the long side into the short side, spread the whole thing out, and make the head prt, and you are done!
