Box

Box origami is very easy to make, and you can put small items inside! Let's give it a try!

  1. Fold the paper in two in the middle and fold it in two again.
  2. Fold the four corners inward.
  3. Fold in from each side toward the center.
  4. Open the top and bottom two sides and fold them toward the center to form a box shape.
