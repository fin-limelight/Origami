Frog

Frog origami is one of the most enjoyable types. Just push the back, and the origami will leap! Let's give it a try!

  1. Fold the paper in half, then fold it in half again.
  2. Add a fold to one side in the shape of an 'X'.
  3. Fold along the crease inward to form a triangle.
  4. Fold the remaining side inward to form a house shape.
  5. Fold the upper part to create the paws, and fold the lower part into a triangle.
  6. Spread the lower part to form a ship shape.
  7. Fold the lower part inward to create the back leg.
  8. Fold in the belly, then fold in the opposite direction, and you're done!
